Welcome

This is the site for the Wildlife Camera Trap

Arduino is used as the platform of this study. A camera trap is being placed in the wild to capture images of animals and upload them here.

Visit Gallery to view the images and their details.












About the Project

The use of camera traps has been a helpful method to monitor and capture different species in a specific area and monitor each of them. With this, the demand of this methodology has dramatically increased in the recent years. In this project, Arduino will be used as a platform to perform the camera trap. A camera will be left in the wild and the captured images will be saved in a database. The saved images will then be accessed through this site.

You can visit Gallery to view the images and their details.
